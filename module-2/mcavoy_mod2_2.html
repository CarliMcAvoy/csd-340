Chapter 1 - The Author

Hi there! My name is Carli McAvoy and I live in the western part of North Carolina. Although it is beautiful here, I can't help but miss my hometown in central Florida from time to time. Being close to the mountains is certainly a change from being close to the beach.

I always tend to brag about my upbringing in Florida. Being so close to the beach, I can't even begin to list all the adventures I used to go on with my friends as a teenager. Not to mention Florida's beautiful natural springs, and of course, their famous amusement parks.

When I'm not thinking about what adventures I can go on next, I have a few hobbies that work as both creative and physical outlets when the world becomes too much to handle. I have always loved creating music, whether it's on the guitar, piano, or singing. When I hit a writer's block, I turn my attention to the beloved gaming consoles. I've also always been very active and find myself boxing, cycling, or hitting the gym when I've got extra energy.

While I may sound like a bit of a free spirit, there is certainly nothing more important to me than my family, blood and chosen. It was my mother's decision to move that originally brought me to North Carolina. Me, my mother, and my sister have had a close relationship my entire life; I couldn't imagine a world without them at the forefront of it.
